Welcome to the Oudah website !

Postdoctoral Researcher at Bordeaux School of Economics and Environnement, Territoires en Transition, Infrastructures, Sociétés (ETTIS, INRAe Bordeaux), Oudah Yobom is an applied and development economist.

My research focuses on the economics of climate change, agricultural economics and economics of food security. Questions related to the economics of development, poverty and the resilience of agricultural households are essential aspects of my work.

Beyond analyzing the effects of climate change on the agricultural sectors (agriculture and food security), I also work on the interactions between economic sectors (urban territories i.e. cities, and firms) with the climate system while providing my expertise on the resilience and adaptation of these economic structures in a context of global change.


Please feel free to contact me for potential collaborations, thematic discussions, questions about my work or invitations to research seminars.
