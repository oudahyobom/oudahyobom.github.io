Research interests

  • Main themes:

    Climate and Applied Economics: Economics of Climate Change, Agricultural Economics and Food Security & Economics of Poverty and Resilience.

    Climate and Business Sectors: Environmental and climate performance of companies, disclosure, reporting and communication on carbon emissions, International corporate carbon data repositories (Carbon Disclosure Project, ISO, Greenhouse Gas Protocol, etc.).

    • Climate and Urban Zones & Territories (i.e. cities): Issues of adaptation and resilience of cities to climate change, KPIs of cities, issue of sustainable and smart cities, city ​​assessment framework (U4SSC, City Protocol, ISO etc.).

  • Methods:

    Applied Econometrics: Macro & Microeconometrics, Impact evaluation, Structural change models, methodology and construction of performance indicators.

    Data Analysis: Analyzes of temporal and spatial data, Principal Component Analysis (PCA), Multiple Correspondence Analysis (MCA), Factor Analysis of Mixed Data (FAMD), Factorial Correspondence Analysis, Multiple Factor Analysis etc.

    Cartography: creation of maps with R and QGIS, set of operations related to the editing of maps, representation and spatial distribution etc.

  • Others fields:

    Finance Climate, Environmental Economics, Environmental Regulation, Microenonomics, Development Macroeconomics, International Economics and Finance, Urban Economy.
